## What Chrysalis is

Chrysalis is a free, open-source Chrome extension that automates syncing account balances from Monarch Money to ProjectionLab. It is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Monarch Money, ProjectionLab, or any of their parent companies.

## Use of unofficial APIs

Chrysalis accesses Monarch Money's internal GraphQL API, which is unofficial and subject to change without notice. This may violate Monarch Money's Terms of Service. You use Chrysalis at your own risk and are responsible for ensuring your use complies with Monarch Money's terms.

Chrysalis uses ProjectionLab's official Plugin API, which is an explicitly supported integration method.
